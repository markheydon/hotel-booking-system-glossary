Guest Check-out

Description

A guest is ready to leave the hotel and checks-out.

  1. The guest request to check-out.
  2. The Receptionist enters/searches the room name(/number).
  3. The system retrieves the details.
  4. The Receptionist deals with any billing issues -- outside the scope of the system.
  5. The guest pays the bill -- again outside the scope.
  6. The user confirms the check-out.
  7. The system records the check-out date/time against the the Booking and changes the status to 'DEPARTED'.
