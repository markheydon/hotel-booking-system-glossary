Booking [Class] (from model)

Description

A booking for one or more Rooms in a Hotel for/by a Guest.

Attributes

bookingDate : Date

Date the booking was originally made.

checkInDate : Date

The date the guest(s) are due to arrive and check-in to the hotel.

checkOutDate : Date

The date the guest(s) are due to check-out of the hotel and leave.

/numberOfNights : Integer

The total number of nights the guest(s) will be staying.
