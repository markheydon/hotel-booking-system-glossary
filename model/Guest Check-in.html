Guest Check-in

Description

A guest arrives at the hotel at the specific time in a booking.

  1. The guest provides the Receptionist with a booking reference number or their name.
  2. The Receptionist enters/searches the details to find the booking.
  3. The system retrieves the booking.
  4. The user confirms the check-in with the Guest to make sure the room types and duration are correct.
    1. Booking details incorrect: The Receptionist would have to potentially cancel and re-book depending on the problem.
  5. The system records the date/time the Guest(s) checked-in to the relevant room and changes the Booking to 'ARRIVED'.
  6. The Receptionist gives the room keys (or other relevant material) to the guest.
